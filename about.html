Who am I?

Hi there! I'm Remy, a passionate event, wedding and portrait photographer based in Gisenyi. I love capturing the special moments that tell your unique story.

My journey into photography began “when I received my first camera at the age of 18”. Since then, I've been fascinated by the power of images to capture emotions and memories. Over the years, my hobby turned into a profession, and I've been fortunate to turn my passion into a career.

With over 2 years of experience, I've had the pleasure of working with amazing clients and on exciting projects. Some of my notable work includes “covering high-profile weddings and collaborating with top fashion brands”.

I am based in Gisenyi with Musanze and offer a range of services including “wedding photography, portrait sessions, and event coverage”. Whether you're looking for a photographer for your big day or a family portrait, I'm here to help.

I'd love to hear from you! Feel free to contact me for a consultation or follow me on social media. Let's create something beautiful together.
